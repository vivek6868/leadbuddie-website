# LeadBuddie — 2 → 10 Paying Customers in 60 Days

**Role:** Solo founder. Limited budget. Goal: 10 paying customers, fastest path.
**Date:** June 2026 · Review weekly.

---

## Assumptions (correct me if wrong)

- Vertical focus: **water purifier / RO dealers doing AMC** (your sharpest wedge — keep it; don't widen).
- Geography: **one city to start** (wherever your 2 current customers are — referrals travel locally).
- You can personally do **done-for-you onboarding** (screen-share Meta WhatsApp setup).
- Pricing live: Free / Starter ₹999 / **Growth ₹2,499 (target plan)** / Scale ₹4,999, with a **30-day free Growth trial**.
- Budget: effectively your time + a few thousand rupees, not paid ad budget.

## The thesis (everything below serves this)

Your product already earns. You're stuck because **the best moment in the product (Buddie replying in the dealer's voice) is locked behind the hardest step (Meta WhatsApp API setup), and you have one proof point.** For the next 60 days you are not a builder — you are a **closer and an onboarder**. The metric that matters is **dealers who have watched Buddie reply to a real customer**, because that number converts to paying almost on its own.

**Target math:** 10 × Growth (₹2,499/mo) ≈ **₹25k MRR**. To get 10 paying you need roughly **20–25 qualified dealers in trial** (≈40–50% trial→paid with done-for-you onboarding). To get 20–25 trials you need **~60–80 real conversations**. That's the whole funnel. Work it backwards.

## 3. Do THIS WEEK (Days 1–7) — exact actions

**Day 1 — Proof.** Call both current customers. Ask 4 questions: How many enquiries/day before vs now? Any AMC renewals you'd have missed? What would you tell another dealer? Can I use your name? Write two 1-paragraph case studies with at least one number each. Get one screenshot of Buddie replying.

**Day 2 — Sharpen the offer.** Rewrite your one-line pitch to outcome, not tech: *"Buddie answers your WhatsApp enquiries in your voice and chases your AMC renewals — free for 30 days, and it never sends a message without your approval."* Update the homepage hero sub-line, pricing page (lead with the free trial), and add the "Approval mode = nothing sends without you" line prominently.

**Day 3 — Build the target list.** List 80 water-purifier/RO dealers you can reach: your city first, distributor groups, IndiaMART sellers, JustDial listings, Kent/Aquaguard/Livpure dealer directories. Name + WhatsApp number + source in a sheet. This list is your 60-day fuel.

**Day 4 — Outbound, version 1.** Message the first 20 dealers personally (not a blast). Short: who you are, one line on what Buddie does, one proof number, "want me to set it up for you free for 30 days — I'll do the setup myself?" Track replies.

**Day 5 — Referral asks.** Ask both current customers for 2 dealer intros each. Offer a clear incentive (e.g. one month free for them per referral that activates). Warm intros convert 5–10× cold.

**Day 6 — Done-for-you onboarding script.** Write the exact 20-minute call flow: screen-share → Meta embedded signup → import their customer list → set AMC plans → switch Buddie to Approval mode → fire the "Try Buddie in your voice" sample. This becomes the repeatable checklist that lets you (later) hand it off.

**Day 7 — Review + book.** Count replies and intros. Book onboarding calls for next week. Target leaving Week 1 with **3–5 onboarding calls scheduled.**

## 4. Do NOT do this month

- ❌ **Refactoring, tests, tech debt, Hutliv cleanup.** None of it moves the 10-customer number.
- ❌ **Hiring or fundraising prep.** Premature. 10 paying customers is the unlock for both.

## 5. The 60-Day Execution Plan

Run it as four 2-week sprints. Each sprint has one job. **North-star number: dealers who've seen Buddie reply to a real customer.**

### Sprint 1 — Days 1–14: Proof + Pipeline
**Job: build the engine, line up trials.**
- Finish 2 case studies + 1 demo asset (Day 1–2).
- Offer reframed around free trial + approval mode (Day 2).
- 80-dealer list built; first 40 contacted (Day 3–7).
- Done-for-you onboarding checklist finalized and tested on 1 real dealer.
- **Targets:** 40 dealers contacted · 6–8 trials started (you onboard each) · **2–3 new paying or committed.**

### Sprint 2 — Days 15–28: Activate + Close
**Job: turn trials into payers, keep contacting.**
- Onboard every trial personally within 24h of signup. No dealer left at the WhatsApp gate alone.
- Contact remaining 40 dealers; follow up the first 40 (most replies come on follow-up #2–3).
- Day-3 and Day-10 trial check-ins: show them their "renewals recovered" number.
- Ship the AMC "revenue at risk" calculator as a shareable link to send cold dealers.
- **Targets:** funnel at ~12–15 trials cumulative · **5–6 total paying.**

### Sprint 3 — Days 29–42: Referral Flywheel
**Job: make existing customers sell for you.**
- Every paying dealer: ask for 2 intros, give the referral incentive.
- Publish the 90-second real demo video; pin it everywhere you talk to dealers.
- Post the case studies in 2–3 dealer WhatsApp/Facebook groups (value-first, not spammy).
- Keep onboarding new trials same-day.
- **Targets:** ≥6 referral intros · **7–8 total paying.**

### Sprint 4 — Days 43–60: Convert the Tail + Systemize
**Job: close the stragglers, lock retention, write the playbook.**
- Hard follow-up on every warm-but-unclosed trial — offer a setup call or a small first-month discount to tip them.
- Win-back any trial that stalled at WhatsApp setup (the #1 drop point) by offering to do it for them live.
- Weekly retention check-in with every live dealer; capture one renewal-revenue number each.
- Write down the repeatable acquisition + onboarding playbook (so customer #11–50 is a process, not a scramble).
- **Targets:** **10 paying customers** · ≥80% of live dealers still active · one documented, repeatable motion.

## 6. Weekly cadence (solo founder, ~limited hours)

Each week, hit these or course-correct:
- **20 new dealers contacted** + follow-ups on prior weeks.
- **Every trial onboarded within 24h**, personally.
- **Every live dealer messaged once** with their results.
- **Ask for at least 2 referrals.**
- **Friday 30-min review:** update the funnel numbers, decide next week's focus.

## 7. The only metrics that matter (ignore the rest)

Track these five in a single sheet. Everything else is vanity.
1. **Dealers contacted** (top of funnel).
2. **Trials started** (and how many you personally onboarded).
3. **Dealers who saw Buddie reply to a real customer** (the activation north star).
4. **Paying customers** (the goal).
5. **Trial→paid conversion %** (tells you if onboarding or offer is the leak).

Ignore: signups without WhatsApp connected, page views, social followers, feature usage breadth, MRR projections.

## 8. The single highest-leverage habit

**Talk to a dealer every working day for 60 days.** Sell, onboard, or check in — but make contact daily. Ten customers is ~60–80 conversations. If you have the conversations, the product closes them. If you hide in the codebase, none of the above happens.

### Bottom line
You don't need more product to get to 10. You need proof, a frictionless first wow, and you personally in the WhatsApp setup seat. Spend 60 days as a closer, not an engineer — then customer #11 onward becomes a system.
