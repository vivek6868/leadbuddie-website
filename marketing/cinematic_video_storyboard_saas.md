## 3. Storyboard Timeline (Second-by-Second)

```mermaid

    Act 1: Quiet Pressure             :active, 00, 10

```

### Act 1 · "Quiet pressure" (0–10s)
*   **0–3s**: Dark cream void. A single iPhone-shaped device fades in, screen dark. Soft ambient pad starts.
*   **3–6s**: Screen lights up. Three WhatsApp message previews drop in one by one:
    *   *🟢 Aquaguard customer · 2 hrs ago · "Hi, what's the price of RO unit?"*
    *   *🟢 Rajesh M · 47 min ago · "Sir, AMC renewal please"*
    *   *🟢 Unknown · 8 min ago · "Filter change needed"*
*   **6–10s**: A "+1", "+2", and "+3" badge pulses on the WhatsApp icon. The screen slowly dims again.
    *   *On-screen Text (7–10s)*: **Every dealer's day, in one screen.**
    *   *Voiceover*: *"Customer enquiries pile up. You can't reach every one."*

### Act 2 · "Buddie wakes up" (10–25s)
*   **10–13s**: The phone tilts gently. A soft purple glow appears at the bottom edge. Buddie's avatar rises into frame in the UI.
*   **13–18s**: Buddie types in real-time. Letters appear one-by-one. Counter badge drops to "0".
    *   *On-screen Text (15–18s)*: **Replies every message. In 10 seconds. In your tone.**
    *   *Voiceover*: *"This is Buddie. Your AI sales employee on WhatsApp."*
*   **18–22s**: Phone rotates 30 degrees. Calendar UI fades in showing four date chips: `60 days` · `30 days` · `15 days` · `7 days` (each gets a green check ✓).
    *   *On-screen Text (19–22s)*: **AMC reminders, automatic. Never miss a renewal.**
*   **22–25s**: Phone rotates, dormant lead list appears. Names (Priya Sharma, Anil Kumar, Sunita Patel) highlight in purple with *"Outreach sent ✓"*.
    *   *On-screen Text (23–25s)*: **Brings back leads you'd forgotten.**
    *   *Voiceover*: *"Replying to leads, sending automatic follow-ups, and retrieving dormant leads — while you focus on what you do best."*

### Act 3 · "You stay in control" (25–35s)
*   **25–30s**: Phone rotates back to center. Approval Desk UI card animates in.
    *   *On-screen Text (28–35s)*: **You read. You approve. Buddie sends. Or let it run on its own when you're ready.**
*   **30–33s**: Cursor hovers on `Approve ✓`, pulses purple, and clicks. Swoosh sound effect.
*   **33–35s**: Two more cards approve themselves in sequence with a clean visual rhythm (tap, swoosh, gone).
    *   *Voiceover*: *"You stay in control. Always."*

### Act 4 · "Logo + offer" (35–45s)
*   **35–40s**: Phone drifts up and out of frame. LeadBuddie logomark fades in, centered, with wordmark below.
    *   *On-screen Text*: **✦ LEADBUDDIE | Your AI Sales Employee on WhatsApp**
    *   *Voiceover*: *"Try Buddie free for 30 days."*
*   **40–43s**: Small badge slides up showing: **30 days free · No card · Made in India**.
*   **43–45s**: Final frame shows message bubble: **↩ Reply YES | LeadBuddie.com**.
    *   *Voiceover*: *"Reply YES — we'll show you on your own dealership data."*

## 4. Production Specifications

*   **Aspect Ratio**: `9:16` vertical format (native for mobile).
*   **Frame Rate**: `24 fps` cinematic look.
*   **Color Palette**: Warm cream (#F5EFE3), accent purple (#6D28D9), subtle 10% film grain.
*   **Music Soundtrack**: Warm electronic ambient (70 BPM, Tycho/Bonobo style). No vocal stems.
*   **Voiceover**: Calm Indian English male (35-45). Conversational tenor, deliberate pacing. 100% English.
